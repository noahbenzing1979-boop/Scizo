# Prismatic Depths Marketing Kit

This folder converts the launch playbook into ready-to-use assets for Google Play, short-form social, community building, influencer outreach, and review operations.

## Files

- `play-store-listing.md`: Google Play title, descriptions, keywords, visual asset plan, A/B tests, and policy notes.
- `launch-calendar.md`: Eight-week pre-launch plan through launch week and first patch.
- `tiktok-content-calendar.md`: Four weeks of short-form video prompts built around press-your-luck moments.
- `influencer-outreach.md`: Micro-influencer target profile, pitch email, DM, follow-up, and build notes.
- `community-plan.md`: Discord, Reddit, and weekly challenge structure.
- `review-and-retention.md`: Review prompt timing, replies, retention hooks, and event ideas.
- `asset-shotlist.md`: Screenshot, trailer, icon, and feature graphic requirements.

## North Star

Every public-facing asset should make one of these instantly clear:

- The player rolls PRISM dice.
- Pressing your luck is the emotional hook.
- Loot cards and neon dungeon rooms are the reward fantasy.
- The game is readable, replayable, and quick to understand.
