## Long Description

Descend into PRISMATIC DEPTHS, a neon dice dungeon forged in the Scizo Engine.

Choose a hero, roll a class-based PRISM dice pool, and decide whether to keep your result or press for a brighter signal. Every room asks the same delicious question: take the safe outcome, or risk the run for a better combo?

Fight strange dungeon threats, survive counterattacks, collect loot cards, bank XP, and push through deeper rooms where rewards get sharper and mistakes get louder.

Features:

- Four hero classes with distinct dice pools and special gifts
- PRISM dice built around damage, guard, focus, combo bonuses, and void risk
- Press-your-luck combat that creates tense, readable decisions
- Monster encounters across escalating depths
- Loot cards that change how your dice behave
- XP banking for safe progress or risky momentum
- Fast solo runs designed for quick mobile sessions
- Neon-on-black dungeon style with clear, high-contrast UI

Prismatic Depths is built for players who like roguelikes, dice games, dungeon crawlers, card loot, tactical risk, and short runs with big moments.

Press your luck. Split the signal. Descend.

## Policy Guardrails

- Keep store copy honest to the current game. Do not show mechanics that are not playable yet.
- Avoid ranking or performance claims such as “best,” “top,” or “#1.”
- Avoid price claims, temporary offers, or “free” language in title or graphics.
- Keep metadata natural. Do not keyword-stuff repeated search terms.
- Screenshots should show the actual app or game experience.

## Source Notes

- Google Play Console Help says app titles must be 30 characters or less and promotional descriptions must be 80 characters or less.
- Google Play preview asset guidance says feature graphics should keep key elements centered, avoid clutter, and use screenshots that depict the actual in-game experience.
