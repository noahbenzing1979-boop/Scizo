# TikTok and Shorts Calendar

Post 3-5 times per week. Keep clips raw, clear, and built around a decision people can understand without knowing the game.

## Format Rules

- Start with the risk in the first second.
- Show the dice, monster HP, or loot result quickly.
- Use captions that describe the decision, not generic hype.
- End on a question: `Would you press?`

## Week 1: Teach the Hook

1. **Would you press this roll?**
   - Show a decent roll.
   - Hover over Press.
   - Cut before result, then reveal in part two or comments.

2. **Four heroes, one bad decision**
   - Show Ironclad, Shade, Arcanist, Warden.
   - Ask which class viewers would pick.

3. **Void backlash hurts**
   - Press into a void.
   - Show HP drop.
   - Caption: `Greed has teeth.`

4. **First loot card**
   - Clear a room.
   - Reveal loot.
   - Caption: `This changes the whole run.`

## Week 2: Clutch Moments

1. **1 HP survival**
   - Show monster HP low and hero HP low.
   - Resolve the roll.

2. **Bank XP or keep going?**
   - Show risk XP.
   - Ask the audience.

3. **The Arcanist combo**
   - Show focus stacking into bonus damage.

4. **Depth II gets rude**
   - Show a stronger monster and higher reward.

## Week 3: Devlog and Personality

1. **Making dice feel good**
   - Show before/after dice UI.

2. **Why press-your-luck works**
   - Explain the feeling in one sentence over gameplay.

3. **Monster naming session**
   - Show `Glass Imp`, `Static Maw`, `Prism Warden`.
   - Ask for monster name suggestions.

4. **Loot card design**
   - Show item text and what it changes.

## Week 4: Pre-Launch Push

1. **Trailer cutdown**
   - 15 seconds: hero, roll, press, fight, loot.

2. **Pick the icon**
   - Show two icon variants.
   - Ask viewers to vote.

3. **Depth Challenge announcement**
   - Define a weekly modifier.

4. **Launch reminder**
   - Short, honest, gameplay-first.

## Comment Reply Prompts

- `You said press. Here is what happened.`
- `Someone asked what Void does. Pain. It does pain.`
- `Trying your hero pick now.`
- `Can this build survive Depth III?`
