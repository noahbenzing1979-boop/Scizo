# Store Asset Shotlist

## Icon

**Concept:** glowing PRISM die mark on a dark neon background.  
**Goal:** readable at tiny sizes. The die silhouette matters more than text.

Checklist:

- No tiny title text.
- Strong cyan/gold/rose contrast.
- Distinct from other RPG and dice icons.
- Works at 48 x 48 px.

## Feature Graphic

**Target:** 1024 x 500 px.

Concept:

- Centered PRISM die above a dungeon grid.
- Cyan and rose glow from the side walls.
- Gold press-your-luck energy around the die.
- Minimal or no text. Let the store title and short description carry the copy.

Do:

- Keep focal content centered.
- Use the same visual language as the game UI.
- Avoid overloading the edges with important details.

Do not:

- Use ranking claims.
- Use price or discount language.
- Put critical text in cutoff zones.

## Screenshot Set

Google Play allows up to eight screenshots per supported device type. For games, prepare at least three strong phone screenshots showing real gameplay.

1. **Hero Select**
   - Caption: `Choose your hero`
   - Show all four hero cards.

2. **Roll Moment**
   - Caption: `Roll PRISM dice`
   - Show chunky dice in the tray.

3. **Press Decision**
   - Caption: `Keep or press your luck`
   - Show roll results and command buttons.

4. **Combat Resolution**
   - Caption: `Break monsters with combos`
   - Show monster HP and run log.

5. **Loot Reward**
   - Caption: `Claim loot cards`
   - Show loot list after a room clear.

6. **XP Banking**
   - Caption: `Bank XP or risk it`
   - Show banked XP and risk XP side by side.

7. **Depth Progression**
   - Caption: `Descend through three depths`
   - Show room and depth status.

8. **Victory or Clutch**
   - Caption: `Survive the final signal`
   - Show a low-HP win or guardian encounter.

## Trailer Structure

Length: 20-30 seconds.

1. 0-3s: hero select and title.
2. 3-8s: dice roll lands.
3. 8-13s: press decision, then reroll.
4. 13-18s: monster takes damage.
5. 18-23s: loot card reward.
6. 23-30s: depth transition and title card.

End card:

`Prismatic Depths: Dice RPG`  
`Press your luck. Split the signal. Descend.`
