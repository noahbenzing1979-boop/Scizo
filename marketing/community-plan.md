# Community Plan

## Discord Structure

Channels:

- `announcements`: builds, patches, launch updates
- `depth-runs`: screenshots and run stories
- `hero-builds`: Ironclad, Shade, Arcanist, Warden theorycrafting
- `loot-lab`: card ideas and balance feedback
- `bug-reports`: structured reports
- `fan-art-and-clips`: social-friendly player moments
- `depth-challenge`: weekly challenge rules and results

## Weekly Depth Challenge

Format:

- One modifier
- One hero focus
- One score target
- One screenshot prompt

Example:

**Static Week**

- Hero: Shade
- Rule: must press at least once per room
- Goal: highest banked XP after Depth II
- Share: screenshot of the best or worst press

## Reddit Plan

Post as a developer, not as an ad.

Good post types:

- `I made a dice dungeon where greed is the main enemy`
- `Would you press this roll?`
- `Before/after UI polish on my indie dice roguelike`
- `Looking for feedback on my first 30 seconds`

Avoid:

- repeated launch spam
- vague hype
- posts with no gameplay
- arguments about feedback

## Community Prompts

- Which hero should get the next special gift?
- Name a monster that punishes greedy players.
- What should a cursed loot card do?
- Would you rather bank 12 XP or press into Depth III?

## Moderation Tone

Keep the community playful, readable, and kind. The game can be intense; the room around it should feel safe to participate in.
