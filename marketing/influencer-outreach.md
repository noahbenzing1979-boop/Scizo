# Influencer Outreach

## Target Profile

Prioritize creators with 5K-50K followers who already post about:

- mobile roguelikes
- dice games
- dungeon crawlers
- indie RPGs
- card battlers
- Android gaming

The best fit is a creator whose videos already explain game decisions out loud.

## Pitch Email

Subject: Neon dice dungeon preview: Prismatic Depths

Hi `Name`,

I’m building **Prismatic Depths: Dice RPG**, a quick-session neon dungeon crawler about rolling PRISM dice, pressing your luck, fighting monsters, and banking XP before the run turns on you.

I thought of your channel because your audience seems to enjoy games where one risky decision can flip the whole run. The build is small and direct: choose a hero, roll, keep or press, resolve combat, claim loot, and descend.

Would you like an early Android build when the next test version is ready?

Useful hooks:

- press-your-luck dice combat
- four hero classes
- loot cards that change dice results
- fast mobile roguelike runs
- neon-on-black visual identity

No pressure to cover it unless it fits your taste. If you do play it, honest feedback is more useful than forced positivity.

Thanks,  
Noah / noahbenzing1979-boop

## Build Package

Send creators:

- APK or Play testing link
- one-paragraph game summary
- 5 bullet hooks
- content-safe music note
- known issues
- contact email
- request for honest feedback

## Creator Brief

Do not ask creators to read a script. Give them moments to chase:

- press a risky roll
- beat a room at low HP
- compare two hero classes
- find a loot card that changes the run
- decide whether to bank XP
